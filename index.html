Message from the director

Nepalgunj Technical College was established in 2009AD (2065 BS) as a privately owned technical college in Nepalgunj. It is located in Nepalgunj-17, Rambag, Rangashala Road, Banke district of Nepal. It is affiliated to Council for Technical Education and Vocational Training (CTEVT) and accredited by Nepal Health Professional Council.
